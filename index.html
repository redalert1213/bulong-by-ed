Your privacy

Bulong does not sell your data. Your Google account is used only to identify you across sessions. You can post anonymously at any time — your name is never required.

Community guidelines

Bulong is a space for honest, vulnerable expression — not hate. Posts that target, threaten, or harass others will be removed. Be the kind of stranger you'd want to find your whisper.

🧠
Mental health disclaimer

Bulong is not a substitute for professional mental health support. Kinig AI is a compassionate listener, not a therapist. If you're in crisis, please reach out to a professional.
